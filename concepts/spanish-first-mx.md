# Spanish-First for Mexican Market

## The rule
Any product targeting Mexican users or businesses must be:
- **Spanish-first** in onboarding, UI, and support
- **MXN-priced** — USD pricing signals "foreign product"
- **RFC/CFDI-aware** if it touches any commercial transaction

## Why
Mexican SMEs and consumers don't trust products that feel foreign. The friction of language switching is enough to kill conversion. SAT compliance (RFC, CFDI 4.0) is a real concern for any B2B product — invoicing is legally required.
