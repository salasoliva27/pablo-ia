### Eyewear / optical (lool-ai)
- Mexico eyewear market: ~$1.93B USD in 2025, growing at 7.2% CAGR through 2035
- LATAM eyewear: $12.89B → $20.56B by 2032. Mexico holds largest LATAM share.
- Virtual try-on global incumbents (Fittingbox, Perfect Corp, Banuba, VARAi, Zakeke) are all English-first, USD-priced, targeting global enterprise — not Mexican optical SMEs
- Gap: Spanish-first, MXN-priced, SME-appropriate B2B SaaS with simple catalog upload
- High-density target neighborhoods for optical SMEs: Roma, Condesa, Polanco, Lomas, Narvarte, Del Valle

### Blockchain / community investment (espacio-bosques)
- Mexican fintech ecosystem: 770+ companies, mature
- Blockchain projects in Mexico increasingly focused on Real World Assets (RWA)
- CNBV and Ley Fintech (2018) govern crowdfunding and investment platforms
- Bosques de las Lomas: high-income residential, ~15,000 residents, HOA-familiar with community fees
- POC-first approach validated: testnet before real funds, validate UX before legal structure
